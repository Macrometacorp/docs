title: Upgrade Plan to Scale
---

You can use the GDN console to upgrade from a free Playground account to Scale tier account. 

1. Log in to your [Macrometa account](https://auth-play.macrometa.io/).
1. Click **Account**.
1. Click **Upgrade Now**.
1. Select **Upgrade** and follow the on-screen instructions to configure your plan and billing information.
